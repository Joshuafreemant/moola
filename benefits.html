Moola Funds Benefits

Moolah Funds are multi-asset instruments which give investors four essential benefits.

Simple

Moolah use funds and indexes to invest with simple and well understood strategies.

Diversified

Multi-asset investing provides diversification – different patterns of performance – which can protect an investor’s return over time, even when markets are volatile.

Cost-effective

Multi-asset investing brings low-cost access to the markets compared to investing in individual assets or actively managed funds.

Risk-managed

Moolah uses institutional risk management tools to protect the portfolios against market, trading and operational risks.

Instrument:

Moola’s Inflation Protected ETF is inspired by the Treasury Protected Inflation Securities (TIPS) funds: it is designed to hedge the official inflation rate.

Strategy:

Moola adjusts the DeFi yields and other incomes to offset the official inflation rate or any more accurate estimate when available. Differently from traditional TIPS, Moola’s funds are effective in the long but also in the short run.
